What happens on a first visit

  1. Short conversation. What brings you in, your history, what you want to get back to.
  2. Assessment. How your body is moving now. Finding out what's real, what's reactive, and what's worth doing something about.
  3. Treatment. Sports massage plus whichever clinical tools make sense for you.
  4. Plan. What to do between sessions, when to come back, and what success looks like.
